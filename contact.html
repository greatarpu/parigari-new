Privacy Policy
1. Information We Collect

When you contact us or submit an Expression of Interest form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any message or motivation you provide. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry or process your application.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to communicate with you regarding your inquiry, application, donation, or volunteer activity. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Sharing

Parigari Social Welfare Trust does not share your personal information with any external organization, agency, or individual, except where required by law or with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All communications through our WhatsApp channel are secured by end-to-end encryption.

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and can be disabled through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by us. To exercise these rights, please contact us at contactus@parigari.org.

7. Changes to This Policy

Parigari Social Welfare Trust reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at contactus@parigari.org or call us at +91 9941309880.
